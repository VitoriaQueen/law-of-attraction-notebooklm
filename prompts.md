## 🧪 Prompt Engineering

### Prompt 1:

Create a 7-item list with the main teachings of Abraham Hicks.

🎯 Object: To create an itemized list with 7 short sentences presenting the main concepts discussed on the sources.
🫤 Unwanted result: Each item presented a longer explanation of the concept.


➡️ Insight: <br /> <br />
###### 1 - **You Are the Creator of Your Own Reality**.<br />
###### 2 - **The Law of Attraction** states that "that which is like unto itself, is drawn".<br />
###### 3 - **The Emotional Guidance System:** Your emotions are your personal GPS from the universe, letting you know whether you are in alignment with your Source Energy.<br />
###### 4 - **The Vortex:** Described as a vibrational "holding space" or "escrow" where everything you have ever asked for already exists energetically.<br />
###### 5 - **The Art of Allowing:** This is the process of reducing vibrational resistance to let your desires manifest.<br />
###### 6 - **The Role of Contrast and Expansion:** Contrast are varied details of life, particularly unwanted situations, which help you clarify what you do want.<br />
###### 7 - **Alignment with Source Energy:** You are a physical extension of a non-physical, eternal Stream of Consciousness called Source Energy. Abraham teaches that nothing is more important than that you feel good,because feeling good is the evidence of your alignment with the purely positive perspective of your Source.
